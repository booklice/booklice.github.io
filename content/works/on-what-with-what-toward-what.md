description: This book reconstructs Franz Kafka’s Metamorphosis. Just as the objects in the novel exert greater power than the subject, the footnotes here overtake the main text, shifting from a subsidiary element to the core of reading. Placed alongside the original text, the footnotes are not mere supplements but interreferential layers that connect in double and triple sequences. Within this accumulation, the reader loses a linear path of reading and instead encounters a field of branching and expansion.
